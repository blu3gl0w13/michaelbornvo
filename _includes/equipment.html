  • Studio

    Every voice-over artist needs propper sound isolation. Michael Born uses a custom built sound isolation booth that exceeds industry standards for sound isolation while maintaining a clean sound. From sound dampening panels that handle reverberation to sound isolation walls that keep sound out. Each delivered performance will eliminate outside noises while maintaining a crisp sounding reverb free sound.

  • Microphones

    Michael maintains an inventory of top of the line industry standard microphones such as the Neumann TLM-103, Shure SM7B, and the Electro-Voice RE20. If your project requires a specific microphone, Michael will do his best to accommodate your specific project requirements.

  • Audio Interface

    Michael Born Voice-Overs uses a Focusrite Scarlett 2i2 Audio Interface and a dbx 286s Microphone Pre-Amp for all recordings. Depending on the microphone used, Michael has the ability to configure these devices in order to maintain the best sound out of each microphone in his inventory.

  • Digital Audio Workstation (DAW)
